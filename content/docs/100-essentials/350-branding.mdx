We’ve prepared this brand toolkit so you’ll know exactly how you can, and cannot display the Kin brand assets.

In this page you’ll find logos, icons, and other assets for fair use and for creating applications & services that benefit the users, developers, and supporters of Kin.

Last Updated: November 2022
